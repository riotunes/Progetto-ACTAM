What's PicTune?

PicTune is a web tool imagined and designed to help to experiment with sound and vision. Coming with an accessible and ready-to-use interface, PicTune allows anyone to play with their recordings with their very own style.


Visualizing sounds

In the middle of the interface there's a window that provides a graphical response to the input recorded from the user. This fusion between visuals and sound (pics and tunes) is the idea that inspired us to build this web app. PicTune analyzes the intensity, noise and rhythm of the sound.
Then, it converts it all into the motion of little and colourful musical particles.


Play with the effects

Pictune is a playground in which you can have fun with four differents built-in effects to apply to your recordings. Experiment to your heart content, turn them on/off, mix them as you like!

You can also choose to manipulate their settings changing their default parameters to better match you musical intentions.


Choose your game mode

You can choose between either using an audio recording device, may it be your laptop integrated microphone or external ones, or using a jacked in instrument to play in MIDI.
It's up to you and to your unique style!
